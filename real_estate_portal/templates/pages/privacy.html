Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our real estate services:

1.1 Personal Information
  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Username and password
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Documents for verification (RERA, identity proof)
1.2 Property Information
  • Property location and address details
  • Property specifications (area, amenities, features)
  • Property photos and videos
  • Price and payment information
  • Property documents and legal information
  • Construction and maintenance details
  • Utility information (water supply, power backup)
1.3 Usage Information
  • Browser type and device information
  • IP address and location data
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Search preferences and saved properties
  • Communication preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and improving our real estate services
  • Processing property listings and transactions
  • Verifying user identity and property ownership
  • Connecting buyers with sellers/agents
  • Sending notifications about properties and services
  • Analyzing market trends and user behavior
  • Ensuring compliance with real estate regulations
  • Personalizing user experience and recommendations
  • Processing payments and maintaining financial records

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Property owners and potential buyers
  • Real estate agents and brokers
  • RERA authorities for compliance
  • Payment processors for transactions
  • Service providers and partners
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure user authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Secure data backups
  • Data breach notification procedures

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Control your privacy settings
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

6. Data Storage and Retention

We retain your information for:

  • Active accounts: Until account deletion
  • Property listings: Duration of listing plus 3 years
  • Transaction records: 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Communication records: 2 years
  • Usage logs: 1 year

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze site usage
  • Personalize content
  • Improve user experience
  • Provide security features

8. Third-Party Services

We integrate with third-party services for:

  • Payment processing
  • Maps and location services
  • Analytics and tracking
  • Social media sharing
  • Document verification

These services have their own privacy policies and data practices.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred and processed in other countries with different data protection laws.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes via:

  • Email notifications
  • Website announcements
  • App notifications
