Smart Tips to Optimize Your Home Loan
  • Make a larger down payment to reduce the principal amount and save on interest
  • Choose an optimal tenure - shorter terms mean higher EMIs but less total interest
  • Compare interest rates from different banks and financial institutions
  • Maintain a good credit score (750+) for better interest rates
  • Consider making prepayments to reduce the principal balance
  • Factor in processing fees and other charges when calculating total cost
Understanding Home Loan EMI Calculation

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is calculated using a standardized mathematical formula that considers the principal amount, interest rate, and loan tenure.

EMI = P × r × (1 + r)^n ÷ ((1 + r)^n - 1)
P = Principal loan amount
r = Monthly interest rate
n = Total number of months
EMI = Monthly payment
